13 - Sculpture WIP, 2018 - Ongoing –

An ongoing project metal sculpture project where I am working with discarded bicycle frames and parts. I am attempting to give these bicycles humanistic qualities that embody their current state of disrepair but to also empower them by repairing them into something beautiful. This project is not yet completed. For the completed version I will repair one or two more bikes, add properly positioned lights, and have video playing. The video will show the bikes current condition before I began to repair them.

Bicycles, CTC Metal Shop for welding, cutting, bending and grinding, iPhone 7, Spotlights
