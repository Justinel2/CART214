12 - Blender Pendant, 2017

Spinning gold pendant was created using a 3D software program called Blender. I applied the spinning pendant to the video-editing program Final Cut Pro 7. The video still was from my own library of footage that I personally filmed. I was able to practice using the green screen editing options offered in Final Cut Pro 7.
